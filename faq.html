Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer? Feel free to contact our customer support team for assistance.

Ordering & Payment

How do I place an order?

To place an order:

  • Browse our products and add items to your cart
  • Click on the cart icon to review your items
  • Click "Proceed to Checkout"
  • Sign in or create an account
  • Enter your shipping and payment information
  • Review your order and click "Place Order"
What payment methods do you accept?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  • PayPal
  • GCash
  • Bank Transfer
  • Cash on Delivery (for eligible areas)
Is my payment information secure?
Yes, we use industry-standard SSL encryption to protect your payment information. We never store your complete credit card details on our servers.

Shipping & Delivery

How long does shipping take?

Our standard shipping times are:

  • Standard Shipping: 3-5 business days
  • Express Shipping: 1-2 business days
  • Same Day Delivery: Within 24 hours (selected areas only)

Delivery times may vary depending on your location and product availability.

Do you ship internationally?
Currently, we only ship within the Philippines. We're working on expanding our international shipping options in the future.
How can I track my order?
You can track your order by:
  • Logging into your account and viewing order details
  • Clicking the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email
  • Contacting our customer service with your order number

Returns & Refunds

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery for most items. The item must be:

  • In original condition
  • Unused and unopened
  • In original packaging with all accessories
  • Accompanied by original receipt or order confirmation
How long do refunds take?
Refunds are typically processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. Credit card refunds may take 7-10 business days to appear on your statement.
Do I have to pay for return shipping?
Return shipping is free for items that are defective or damaged upon arrival. For other returns, a return shipping fee may apply.

Product Information

Are your products authentic?
Yes, all products sold on TechGadgetHub are 100% authentic and come with manufacturer warranties. We only source products from authorized distributors and manufacturers.
Do you offer product warranties?

Yes, our products come with:

  • Manufacturer's warranty (varies by product)
  • Our 14-day return policy
  • Extended warranty options available at checkout
How do I know if a product is in stock?
Product availability is shown on each product page. If an item is out of stock, you can:
  • Sign up for stock notifications
  • Check back later
  • Contact our customer service for estimated restock dates

Account & Security

How do I create an account?

To create an account:

  • Click on "Account" in the top right corner
  • Select "Create Account"
  • Fill in your details
  • Verify your email address
  • Start shopping!
How do I reset my password?

To reset your password:

  • Click on "Account"
  • Select "Forgot Password"
  • Enter your email address
  • Follow the instructions in the reset email
How do I update my account information?

You can update your account information by:

  • Logging into your account
  • Going to "Account Settings"
  • Updating the information you want to change
  • Saving your changes
