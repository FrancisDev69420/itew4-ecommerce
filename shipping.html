Shipping Policies

How do I track my order?
Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track your package by clicking the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email or by visiting our tracking page.
What if my package is damaged?
If you receive a damaged package, please take photos of the damage and contact our customer service within 24 hours of delivery. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund.
Do you ship internationally?
Currently, we only ship within the Philippines. We're working on expanding our shipping services to other countries soon.
What are your shipping cut-off times?
Orders placed before 2:00 PM (Philippine Time) will be processed on the same day. Orders placed after 2:00 PM will be processed on the next business day.

Delivery Areas

We currently deliver to all major cities and provinces in the Philippines. For remote areas, delivery times may vary. Please contact our customer service for specific delivery information to your location.
