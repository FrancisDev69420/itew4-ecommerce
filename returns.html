Returns & Exchanges

Important: All returns must be initiated within 14 days of delivery. Items must be in original condition with all packaging and accessories.

Return Policy

Eligibility

We accept returns and exchanges for most items within 14 days of delivery, provided that they are:

  • In original condition
  • Unused and unopened
  • In original packaging with all accessories
  • Accompanied by original receipt or order confirmation

Items Not Eligible for Return:

  • Opened software or digital products
  • Personalized or custom-made items
  • Items marked as "Final Sale"
  • Products with removed security seals
  • Items purchased from third-party sellers

Return Process

1

Initiate Return

Log into your account and select the order you wish to return. Click "Return Item" and follow the prompts.

2

Package Item

Pack the item securely in its original packaging with all accessories and documentation.

3

Print Label

Print the return shipping label provided in your return confirmation email.

4

Ship Item

Drop off the package at your nearest shipping center or schedule a pickup.

Refund Information

Processing Time

Once we receive your return:

  • Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • Credit card refunds may take 7-10 business days to appear on your statement
  • You will receive an email confirmation when your refund is processed

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do I have to return an item?
You have 14 days from the delivery date to initiate a return. The item must be shipped back within 7 days of initiating the return.
Do I have to pay for return shipping?
Return shipping is free for items that are defective or damaged upon arrival. For other returns, a return shipping fee may apply.
Can I exchange an item instead of returning it?
Yes, you can request an exchange during the return process. If the new item costs more, you'll need to pay the difference. If it costs less, you'll receive a refund for the difference.
What if I received a damaged item?
If you receive a damaged item, please take photos of the damage and contact our customer service within 24 hours of delivery. We'll arrange for a replacement or refund.
